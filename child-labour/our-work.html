Our Work

Child labor is a serious issue that needs to be addressed. As a parent, it is important to be aware of the potential dangers and to take action to prevent your child from becoming a victim of child labor. Start by educating your children on the reality of child labor and the potential risks and dangers associated with it. Set firm boundaries and reinforce them by providing a safe and supportive home environment. Ensure that your children are not being taken advantage of by employers and be mindful of any signs of abuse. Also, make sure that your children are not working in dangerous or hazardous environments, and that they have access to adequate healthcare and education. Finally, be vocal about your stance on child labor and encourage others to do the same.
