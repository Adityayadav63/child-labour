Our Impact

Child Labour prevention has a significant impact on both children and society. It gives children the chance to pursue their education, which can provide them with the skills and knowledge necessary to escape poverty and lead a more fulfilling life. Furthermore, it can help reduce the cycle of poverty and create more opportunities in the labour market, as more children are able to contribute to the economy through skilled and educated work. By preventing Child Labour, there are also positive impacts on society, as it allows children to have a voice and be involved in social and political activities, which can lead to greater awareness of human rights abuses and better protection of vulnerable children. Finally, Child Labour prevention helps ensure that children are not exposed to hazardous and dangerous working environments, which can have long-term implications for their health and safety.
